OUR STORY

Meet the Team

The Founder

Davilla Johnson – Founder & Head Baker

With a passion for fresh, homemade bread and a dream to bring warmth to every home,
Davilla started this bakery with one simple goal: to make every loaf feel like a hug.
With years of experience in baking and a heart full of love, she ensures that every
product is made with the finest ingredients and the utmost care.

The Team Behind the Magic

It takes a dedicated team to keep the bakery running smoothly! From early-morning
bakers crafting fresh loaves to friendly staff members ensuring every customer
leaves with a smile, our small but mighty team is here to serve you.

James & Anita– Master Bakers

Kneading, rolling, and baking with love every day.

.

Sophia – Customer Happiness Manager

Ensuring every visit feels like home.

.

Mike – Delivery & Logistics

Bringing fresh bread straight to your doorstep.
